About Me

Photographer | Editor | Designer | Outdoor Enthusiast

Britt Moore is a freelance photographer and videographer with five years of experience in the industry. A former motocross racer, she realized that she is better suited behind the lens than chasing the checkered flag. In 2012 Britt picked up a DSLR for the first time and began her career, later expanding outside the world of motocross. Passionate about all forms of athletics, Britt pursues other sports in her free time such as rock climbing, mountain biking and surfing.
